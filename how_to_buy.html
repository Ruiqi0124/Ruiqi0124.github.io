How to Buy

Step 1: Browse our Inventory

Visit our Inventory page to explore the available JDM cars. You can view detailed information about each car, including specifications, photos, and pricing.

Step 2: Contact Us

Once you have found a car you are interested in, contact us using the information provided on the Contact Us page. Our team will be happy to assist you with any questions and guide you through the buying process.

Step 3: Arrange a Visit

If you would like to see the car in person before making a purchase, we welcome you to visit our warehouse in Osaka. You can find more information about our location and working hours on the About us page.

Step 4: Make a Purchase

Once you have made your decision, our team will help you complete the necessary paperwork and guide you through the payment process. We offer secure and reliable payment options to ensure a smooth transaction.

Step 5: Import and Delivery

After the purchase is finalized, we will handle the import process and arrange for the delivery of your JDM car. Our team has extensive experience in international shipping to ensure your car reaches you safely and efficiently.

If you have any further questions or need assistance at any step of the buying process, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are dedicated to providing excellent customer service and helping you drive your dream car!
